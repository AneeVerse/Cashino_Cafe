What problems does monjur solve?

Bad or Missing Contracts

MSPs often operate with old agreements or none at all, leaving them exposed to unnecessary risks and liabilities.

Lack of Vendor Risk Protection

Without proper waivers, MSPs remain vulnerable to lawsuits stemming from third-party service provider actions or omissions.

No Way to Update

Traditional contracts make it difficult to implement crucial updates across your client base, potentially leaving you with outdated terms.
